02 / ABOUT

READING WHAT EXISTS BEFORE THE IMAGE.

CheckGlyphs begins from an interest in transduction: the passage of information from one form into another.

It looks beneath the final image for hidden alphabets: structures, rules, and relations that can be read, preserved, and given another form.

It does not restyle Checks. It translates their chromatic behavior into a deterministic glyph system.
